Facts about carbon emitters

  1. Per day a cruise emits as much carbon as 84000 cars
  2. A commuter driving 40 km a day to and back from work in a mid-range car emits 1.2 tons of carbon a year
  3. If possible try travelling by train rather than by plane to emit only 1/5 of the Carbon
  4. In a household switching to green energy saves up to 1 ton of carbon emissions
  5. Heating is responsible for 70% of a households emissions
  6. Data centers through which internet runs worldwide emit as much carbon as international air traffic
  7. In 2015 the textile industry produced 1.2 billion tons of carbon - more than the international air and ship traffic together
  8. 100g of beef equal 8km of a car drive
  9. Cows produce 150 billion gallons of methane per day -> Methane has a global warming potential 86 times that of CO2 on a 20 year time frame
  10. A plant based diet cuts your carbon footprint by 50%
